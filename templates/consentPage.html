Consent Form

You are invited to participate in a research study. This study is being conducted by Assistant Professor Roy Lee Ka-Wei from Singapore University of Technology and Design.
The purpose of this study is to investigate the effectiveness and persuasiveness of large language model in explaining social media posts. The survey will take approximately 10 mins of your time. You will be asked to complete an online survey that contains questions about the hatefulness of a Twitter tweet message and four questions about the quality of the provided explanation (if applicable).

Your participation in this study is completely voluntary and you have the right to withdraw from participation at any time without giving any reason and penalty. You may decline to answer any survey questions, and end your participation. If you do not wish to complete this survey, just close your browser and all your responses will be discarded.
